Photo Quests

YOUR CAMERA IS YOUR TOOL

NPCs request specific objects — "a red metal mailbox" or "a green wooden bench." Find it in the real world. Photograph it. AI verifies instantly and you collect materials: wood, metal, stone, organic, tech, culture. Use those resources to upgrade your base and expand your territory.

GPS Quests

WALK THERE. COMPLETE THE MISSION.

Each NPC marks a destination on your real-world map. Walk there. Arrive. Complete their unique minigame — crack a rusted lock for Juno, track a Krawler for Doc Harlow, decode a signal for Crank, or recover a lost memory for Lumen. The further you venture from your base, the bigger the reward.

// The Gameplay Loop

1. Open Klaim. Choose your mission — photograph an object or walk to a GPS destination.

2. Step outside. Hunt for the object, track a Krawler, or navigate to a salvage drop.

3. Complete the quest. AI verifies your photo — or arrive and play the NPC's minigame.

4. Collect materials. Upgrade your base. Expand your territory. Level up.

Photo quests and GPS quests every day. Free. More with premium. Every walk builds your empire.
